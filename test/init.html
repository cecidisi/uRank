(or follow this link)





The task consists in exploring a collection of documents and finding the ones that most interest you.


  1. Click on the link at the bottom to open uRank
  2. Select any topic from the dropdown list (free choice)
  3. Use keyword tags to explore the collection, as shown in the video
  4. When you find interesting keywords, drag and drop tags to rank the document list
  5. Add the most interesting documents to your bookmarks (recommended between 5 and 10)
  6. When you're done, click on Finish (please don't forget to do it, otherwise we can't collect action logs)
  7. Please follow the instrcutions to fill a post-study questionnaire
